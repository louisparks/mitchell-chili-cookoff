What constitutes chili?

Defining chili can be tricky, but rest assured, the official judges and the community will know it when they taste it. If it walks like chili, smells like chili, and tastes like chili, you are probably on the right track.

What are the competition categories?

Chilis will be judged in the following categories:

  • Best Overall (1st Place and 2nd Place)
  • Best Veg
  • Most Original
  • Spiciest
  • Best Name

How much chili should I make?

You do not want to run out and cut your votes short. A good rule of thumb is two to four pounds of protein per entry, adjusted as needed for vegetarian chilis. Plan generously so plenty of people can taste and vote.

Can I enter more than one chili?

Each contestant may enter one chili unless otherwise announced. This helps keep judging fair and tasting manageable.

Do I have to be a professional cook?

Absolutely not. This event is about fun, creativity, and community. Home cooks are encouraged and often win.

Is there a cost to enter as a contestant?

There is no cost to enter a chili, but all contestants must purchase an attendee ticket.

Who gets to vote?

All attendees get a vote in the People's Choice categories, while official judges will select winners in their designated categories.
